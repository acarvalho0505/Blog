How Happy is Your Country? The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness.The World Happiness 2016, which ranks 155 countries by their happiness levels, was released at the United Nations at an event celebrating International Day of Happiness on March 20th. The goal of this data is to study what factors policy-makers can employ to increase the happiness of their country’s population. Thanks to Kaggle, this data was provided in an easily accessible data set!
